# Bigg Boss 19: Balraj Singh Confirms Sending Legal Notice To Tanya Mittal; Says ‘She Tried To Defame Me’ – EXCLUSIVE

Published Date: Tuesday September 09, 2025

In an exclusive conversation with Zoom/Telly Talk India, Balraj Singh confessed that he has sent legal notice against Bigg Boss 19 fame Tanya Mittal. Read full story to know more.

Balraj Singh had an exclusive interaction with Zoom/Telly Talk India. He confirmed sending legal notice to Bigg Boss 19 contestant. Balraj claimed that Tanya tried to defame him.

Bigg Boss 19 contestant Tanya Mittal has been making headlines with her statements in the Salman Khan show. Many netizens troll her for her behaviour in the madhouse. However, Balraj Singh, who claims to be her boyfriend, mentioned that Tanya Mittal used him to get popularity. Balraj exclusively told Zoom/Telly Talk India that he has sent legal notice to her.

Balraj Singh sends legal notice to Tanya Mittal. (Credit: Instagram)

Balraj Singh slams Tanya Mittal

Balraj Singh told us that he has sent legal notice to Tanya Mittal’s team and asked them to remove his videos from social media. He said, “She did a podcast with me. We clicked a picture, which she later presented in a different manner by adding a romantic song, 'Humare Milan Ki Ghadi' to it. I ignored that thing initially, but I can't ignore it now. I have already sent a legal notice to her and asked her team to remove my videos with her. They can't put my videos without my consent.”

Balraj Singh also stated that he can file a defamation case too. He said, “I can file a defamation case against Tanya. She tried to defame me. When she questions me after coming out, I will definitely give her a strong reply.”

Talking about Balraj Singh, he is quite upset with Tanya Mittal for allegedly using him for her benefits. On the other hand, his ex-girlfriend, Zoya Khan claimed that Balraj and Tanya were in a relationship. She stated, “When Tanya entered Bigg Boss, he couldn’t digest it. He’s been trying everything to get into the show himself, but it hasn’t worked. Last time, he even targeted Armaan Malik, but when that didn’t help, he later went to Armaan’s house and apologised.”

Currently, Tanya Mittal has been actively participating in the game to make her presence felt in the Bigg Boss 19 house. Stay tuned for more updates!

# Bigg Boss 19: Balraj Singh Claims Tanya Mittal 'Utilised' Him; Says 'Jaane Se Pehle Bhi...' - EXCLUSIVE

In an exclusive chat with Zoom/ Telly Talk, Balraj Singh alleged that his rumoured ex-girlfriend Tanya Mittal 'utilised' him. The content creator is currently in Bigg Boss 19 house.

Tanya Mittal is in the spotlight again! The _Bigg Boss 19_ contestant has courted controversy because of her rumoured ex-boyfriend, Balraj Singh. He is now a sarpanch and has been busy throwing shade at the reality show contestant. In fact, he recently interacted with _Zoom_ /_Telly Talk_. In conversation with us, Balraj claimed that his alleged ex-girlfriend 'utilised' him not once but twice in life.

In an interview with us, Balraj said, "_Mujhe lagta hai usne uss samay bhi mera utilise kia tha aur jaane se pehle bhi mera utilise karke nikal gayi, sorry bolke aur emotionally attach karke. Kyunki vo jaanti thi Balraj pe vaar kaha se karna hai aur vo karke gayi_."

(Loose translation: I feel she used me earlier and before going to the Bigg Boss 19 house. She apologised and made emotionally attached. Because she knew where to attack Balraj and she did that)

He further recalled meeting Tanya in Vrindavan. "_Humne Vrindavan milne ka plan banaya kyunki vo Vrindavan aayi hui thi. Main Vrindavan gaya Mumbai se. Pohochne ke baad hum mile. Radisson Blu hotel mein vo ruki hui thi. Vaha pe main baitha jaa kar. Toh apni team ke saath niche ek cameraman, aur do guards the lekin maine itna vaha pe parakhne ki koshish nahi ki kyunki main unse pehle mulakat kar raha tha toh._"

(Loose translation: We planned to meet in Vrindavan because she was already there. I visited Vrindavan from Mumbai. We met at Radisson Blu hotel where she stayed. Her team, one cameraman and two guards were present. But I didn't test them as I was meeting her for the first time)
