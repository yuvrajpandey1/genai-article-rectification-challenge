# Akshay Kumar’s 58th Birthday Special Post Is All About Love And Gratitude: Over 150 Films And Counting...

Published Date: Tuesday September 09, 2025

Akshay Kumar has shared a special post on his 58th birthday. Fans have started showering love and wishes on him. Read full story to know more

Akshay Kumar is celebrating his 58th birthday today (September 9), and on this special occasion, the superstar has been receiving a lot of love and wishes from his fans. Akshay has been working in the film industry for 34 years. Hence, on this birthday, Akshay Kumar shared a special post on Instagram, in which he summarised his life while expressing love and gratitude towards his well-wishers.

## Akshay Kumar’s birthday post

Akshay Kumar shared a special picture that shows him with the characters played by him in the background. Akshay penned a long note in the caption.

Akshay captioned the post as, “Good morning, everyone! 58 years in the making, 34 years in this industry, over 150 films & counting. To everyone that ever believed in me, who bought a ticket, who signed me, produced me, directed me and guided me, this is your journey as much as mine. I’m just here to say an eternal ‘Thank You’ for every kind action, unconditional support, and words of encouragement. I am nothing without you, my Birthday is a dedication to all those that still believe me. Love & Prayers, Your Akshay Jai Mahakal.”

Akshay also thanked Rahul Nanda for specially creating a picture. He added, “Ps: Many thanks to the very talented Rahul Nanda for capturing my life’s work for my favourite people on the planet, my Fans.”

Akshay Kumar’s post is going viral on social media and netizens can’t stop reacting to it. One user wrote, “Happy Birthday My Dear Akki.” Another fan said, “Happy Birthday Khiladi Kumar.” One more fan stated, “Happy birthday Akshay Kumar! You're a true inspiration to us all.”

Talking about Akshay Kumar, the actor will next be seen in Jolly LLB 3. The film also stars Arshad Warsi, Saurabh Shukla and others in key roles. Jolly LLB 3 is releasing on September 19, 2025 in theatres.
