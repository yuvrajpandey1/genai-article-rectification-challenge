# Asia Cup 2025 prize money: Check what the winners and runners-up will receive

Published Date: Tuesday September 09, 2025

The Asia Cup 2025 will begin on September 9 with Afghanistan facing Hong Kong in Abu Dhabi. India will open their campaign on September 10 against hosts United Arab Emirates at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium. The tournament will conclude on September 28. This edition will be played in the T20 format, serving as preparation for the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup next year in India and Sri Lanka.

According to reports, the winners of the 2025 Asia Cup are expected to receive USD 300,000 (around Rs 2.6 crore). The runners-up will get USD 150,000. An official confirmation from the Asian Cricket Council (ACC) is awaited, but the proposed amount would be an increase from the previous edition.

Eight teams will compete for the title.

1. India
2. Pakistan
3. Sri Lanka
4. Bangladesh
5. Afghanistan 
6. UAE
7. Oman
8. Hong Kong

India, who have won the Asia Cup a record eight times, enter the tournament as defending champions. The team will play under the leadership of Suryakumar Yadav in the absence of Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma.

After their opener against UAE, India will meet Pakistan on September 14 in Dubai, a match expected to draw wide attention. Their final group stage match will be against Oman on September 19 in Abu Dhabi.

The top two sides from each group will qualify for the Super Four stage, starting September 20. Teams in the Super Four will play three games each in Dubai, and the top two will move to the final scheduled for September 28.
